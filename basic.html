How To Use

Redstone is a fairly simple concept to understand. Redstone can be divided into two modes, on and off. To turn redstone on, you need something to power it, like a lever, torch, or button. However, this signal will eventually run out, and thats where repeaters come in. Repeaters can restore a redstone circuit's signal if any signal runs through it.
